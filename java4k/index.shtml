Whack-a-Square

A whack-a-sqare game in a couple of KB of code; it doesn't use any fancy bytecode tricks, it's just a simple game :P

Click the game to give it focus

Use the number pad, numlock must be OFF

Every (300 - (hits*3) - (misses*10)) ms the blocks get a 1/20 chance of changing, miss 10 and the game starts over
